Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping options?

We offer free shipping on orders above ₹2,999 across India. Standard delivery takes 5-7 business days, and express delivery is available for urgent orders.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Return shipping is free for defective items.

Do you offer custom designs?

Yes! We work with our artisan partners to create custom pieces. Contact us with your requirements, and we'll provide a quote and timeline.

Do you have a physical store?

Currently, we operate online-only to keep our prices accessible. However, we're planning to open experience centers in major cities soon.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and digital wallets. We also offer EMI options on orders above ₹5,000.

Are your products eco-friendly?

Sustainability is core to our brand. We use natural materials, eco-friendly packaging, and work with artisans who follow sustainable practices.
